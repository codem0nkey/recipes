Ingredients:
2 handfuls of tortilla chips
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
sliced jalapeños

Directions:
  1. Evenly spread out tortilla chips onto a microwaveable plate
  2. Evenly spread shredded cheddar cheese on top of tortilla chips
  3. Evenly place jalapenos onto the cheddar cheese
  4. Heat plate in the microwave on high for 45 seconds
